Founded in 2020, USC MEMO is the fourth chapter of the nonprofit M.E.M.O. organization. We collaborate with our sister chapters (UCI, UCSD, UCLA) and host local volunteer events and pre-health development programs.

Our Focus Areas:

  • Volunteer: Hygiene kits, health fairs, educational materials
  • Professional Development: Panels, workshops, info sessions
  • Social: Big-little mentorship, bonding, and support
“M.E.M.O. has had the most significant impact on my college career... what makes it so special is the family that all MEMObers are a part of.” — Catherine Do, '21
