Each year, USC MEMO travels to underserved areas of Vietnam with physicians and student volunteers to operate mobile clinics and deliver medical and educational support.

Volunteers Assist With:

  • Patient Intake & Triage
  • Ultrasound & EKG
  • Pharmacy & Prescription Pickup
  • Dental Care & Oral Hygiene

Student volunteers receive training before departure and work directly with health professionals on-site.
